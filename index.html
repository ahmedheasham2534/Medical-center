Cardiology

Our Cardiology Department offers expert care for heart-related conditions. With advanced diagnostic tools and treatments

Our experienced cardiologists provide personalized care for managing heart disease, hypertension, arrhythmias, and more. From preventive care to complex procedures, we are dedicated to improving your heart health and ensuring your overall well-being.

Advanced Care for Neurological Health

Our Neurology Department specializes in diagnosing and treating disorders of the nervous system. With advanced technology and expert neurologists

We provide personalized care for conditions such as stroke, epilepsy, migraines, and neurodegenerative diseases. We are committed to improving brain and nerve health, enhancing your quality of life with comprehensive neurological care

Expert Care for Liver and Digestive Health

Our Hepatology Department specializes in diagnosing and managing liver, pancreas, and biliary diseases.

With advanced diagnostics and personalized treatment plans, we provide expert care for conditions such as hepatitis, fatty liver, cirrhosis, and digestive disorders, ensuring optimal health and improved quality of life.

Comprehensive Care for Children's Health

Our Pediatrics Department focuses on the health and well-being of children, offering expert care from infancy through adolescence

From routine check-ups and vaccinations to managing illnesses and developmental concerns, our compassionate team ensures your child grows healthy, happy, and strong in a nurturing environment.

Vision Health for a Brighter Future

Our Eye Care Department provides comprehensive services for maintaining and improving vision.

From routine eye exams and vision correction to treating conditions like cataracts, glaucoma, and retinal disorders, our skilled specialists use advanced technology to ensure the best outcomes for your eye health and overall well-being.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services, doctors, and facilities

What services does Medical Center offer?

We provide comprehensive healthcare services, including general check-ups, specialized consultations, diagnostics, and treatments.

How can I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment online via our website or contact us directly.

Do you accept health insurance?

Yes, we accept a variety of major health insurance plans. If you’re unsure whether your plan is covered, please reach out to us, and our team will assist in verifying your insurance.

Are your doctors specialized in their fields?

Our medical team comprises highly experienced specialists across diverse fields such as cardiology, pediatrics, neurology, and more. Each doctor is dedicated to providing personalized care with expertise in their area.

What are your working hours?

We are open 24 hours, ensuring convenient access to our services. In addition, our emergency services are available 24/7 to handle urgent medical needs at any time.

Can I access my medical records online?

Yes, all patients can securely access their medical records through our user-friendly online portal. Registration is required, and you can easily track your health history, test results, and appointments.
